cost R1550.00 per night


The Presidential Suites are between 166sqm and 332sqm and are located on the 18th, 19th and 20th floors of the hotel and have a separate lounge, dining room, guest toilet and bedroom with en-suite bathroom. Each room features a home automation system that manages the lights, televisions and curtains as well as a telescope that is perfect for stargazing. The large windows offer views of the cityscape. The presidential suite located on the 20th floor consists of two levels with an outdoor dining area, Jacuzzi and private bar.
